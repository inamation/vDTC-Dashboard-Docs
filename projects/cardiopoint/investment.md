# CardioPoint — Investment Details

## The Problem

Cardiac events kill 350,000 Americans annually outside the hospital — most within the first hour. Paramedics acquire 12-lead ECGs but lack real-time AI interpretation. Hospital cardiology teams receive data too late to intervene at the point of care. The diagnostic gap between field acquisition and clinical decision costs lives.

---

## The Opportunity

The global cardiac monitoring market was $5.8B in 2023, growing at 7.2% CAGR. Remote patient monitoring adoption accelerated post-pandemic. AI-assisted ECG interpretation is now FDA-cleared in multiple predicate devices, establishing regulatory precedent. The pre-hospital segment is underserved — no existing product delivers real-time AI arrhythmia interpretation from field acquisition through clinical handoff.

---

## Product Economics

### CardioPoint Hub — Hospital / PSAP
**Market:** ~6,000 hospitals with cardiac programs in the US
**Pricing model:** SaaS — $18,000–$36,000/year per facility
**COGS estimate:** ~$2,400/year (cloud compute + support)
**Gross margin target:** 85%+

### CardioPoint Field Unit — EMS / Ambulance
**Market:** ~15,000 EMS agencies, ~60,000 transport vehicles
**Pricing model:** Hardware + subscription — $1,800 device + $1,200/year
**COGS estimate:** ~$400 device + $200/year
**Gross margin target:** 78%+ (hardware), 83%+ (recurring)

### CardioPoint Home — Consumer Monitoring
**Market:** 34M Americans 65+, 15M with monitored cardiac conditions
**Pricing model:** Device + subscription — $249 + $14.99/month
**COGS estimate:** ~$80 device + $2.50/month
**Gross margin target:** 68%+ (device), 83%+ (subscription)
