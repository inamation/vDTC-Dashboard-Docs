## Clinical Validation Plan

- **Dataset:** PhysioNet MIT-BIH Arrhythmia Database (48 records, 30+ arrhythmia types)
- **Sensitivity target:** ≥95% for AF, VF, VT; ≥90% for STEMI/NSTEMI
- **Specificity target:** ≥90% to limit false alarms in clinical workflow
- **Clinical trial sites:** Target: 2 academic medical centers, 1 EMS agency
