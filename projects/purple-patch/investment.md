# Purple Patch — Investment Details

## The Problem

Chronic wound care is a $25B/year problem in the US alone — driven by diabetes (DFU), venous leg ulcers, pressure injuries, and surgical site infections. Current dressings are passive: a nurse changes them every 2–3 days, records a visual assessment, and waits for lab cultures if infection is suspected. The 48–72 hour culture lag allows wounds to deteriorate before intervention. There is no FDA-cleared real-time wound bioelectric monitoring device.

---

## The Opportunity

**Global wound care market:** $22B in 2023, growing at 5.8% CAGR
**Active/smart wound care segment:** $850M in 2023, growing at 19% CAGR — fastest segment
**Diabetic foot ulcer market alone:** $4.2B globally; 1.4M DFU patients in US requiring advanced wound care/year
**Reimbursement precedent:** CMS reimburses advanced wound dressings under HCPCS A-codes; biosensor reimbursement pathway via CPT 99091 (remote monitoring)

---

## Product Economics

### Purple Patch Active Bandage — Acute (Hospital/Wound Clinic)
- Pricing: $180–$280 per dressing kit (sensor + reservoir; 7-day wear)
- COGS estimate: ~$38 per kit at scale (flexible PCB, STM32, BLE module)
- Gross margin target: 79%+
- Application: 1.4M DFU patients × 26 dressing changes/year = 36M dressings/year TAM

### Purple Patch Monitor — Remote Patient Monitoring
- Pricing: $28/month SaaS (data platform + alerts + EHR integration)
- Addressable: 3M chronic wound patients in remote monitoring programs
- COGS: $3.50/month
- Gross margin target: 87.5%+
