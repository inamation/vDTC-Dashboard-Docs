# 911SchoolBUS

**Safety That Rides With Them, Because They're Someone's Everything.**

School buses and city transit vehicles represent the largest daily mass-gathering environments for children and civilians. 911 School eBus transforms every bus into an intelligent emergency response node — with onboard medical detection, driver alert systems, and direct 911Hub integration.

---

## Core Technology

- **Onboard sensor array** — air quality, CO/smoke detection, passenger count via computer vision
- **Medical emergency detection** — seat pressure + motion anomaly for unconscious passenger detection
- **Driver alert system** — dashboard alert + haptic seat for emergency events; auto 911Hub notification
- **VitalsOne integration** — student wristband data relayed through bus BLE gateway
- **Fleet command dashboard** — real-time location, passenger count, and incident status for district administrators
- **Automatic door unlock** — emergency responder access protocol on 911Hub alert confirmation

---

## Target Markets

- K–12 school district fleets
- Municipal transit authorities
- Private charter and tour bus operators
