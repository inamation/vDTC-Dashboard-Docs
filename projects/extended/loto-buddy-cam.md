# LOTObuddyCAM

**Your Backup When No One Else Is There.**

LOTO BuddyCam is a smart wearable camera integrated into a lockout/tagout safety tag. Designed for solo maintenance technicians, it enables live video monitoring, AI hazard detection, and automatic emergency escalation — providing a virtual safety buddy for lone workers in industrial environments.

---

## Core Technology

- **LOTO tag integration** — camera, mic, and alert button built into standard lockout/tagout tag form factor
- **Live video stream** — 1080p; BLE relay to facility Wi-Fi or LTE; monitored by safety supervisor dashboard
- **AI hazard detection** — arc flash risk, unexpected movement, posture anomaly (fall detection)
- **Lone worker alert** — motion timeout triggers automatic check-in prompt; no response escalates to 911Hub
- **911Hub integration** — incident alert with GPS location and live video feed to PSAP
- **Battery life** — 12-hour shift-length operation

---

## Target Users

- Solo industrial maintenance technicians
- Electrical and mechanical shutdown crews
- Mining and confined space workers
- Facilities management in high-hazard environments
