# 911EscapeMask

**Innovation You Can Breathe In.**

EscapeMask™ is a modular multi-threat personal respiratory system for floods, hurricanes, wildfires, chemical releases, riots, and fires. It is the first consumer-grade escape respirator with integrated air quality sensing, GPS, and 911Hub emergency escalation.

---

## Core Technology

- **Multi-threat filtration** — P100 particulate + activated carbon for CBRN threat spectrum; 60-minute protection
- **Modular cartridge system** — snap-in filter replacement; configurable for wildfire smoke, chemical, or biological threat
- **Integrated sensors** — air quality (CO, VOC, O₂ depletion); real-time display on mask HUD
- **911Hub link** — mask activation triggers location ping to 911Hub; air quality data relayed for dispatcher awareness
- **Universal fit** — one-size adjustable; compatible with prescription eyewear; NIOSH-pending certification
- **Compact storage** — belt pouch format; 5-second donning

---

## Target Users

- Residential emergency preparedness
- First responder personal protective equipment
- Industrial and hazmat environments
- Military and law enforcement
