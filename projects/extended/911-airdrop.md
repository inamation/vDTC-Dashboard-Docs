# 911AirDrop

**HAB — Rapid-Deploy Shelter Infrastructure System**

911AirDrop™ is a scalable, air-deployable, long-term housing and infrastructure system capable of transforming disaster relief, military forward operating bases, and humanitarian response through high-altitude balloon (HAB) and air-drop logistics.

---

## Core Concept

- **HAB delivery system** — high-altitude balloon platform for precision drop of shelter modules to GPS coordinates
- **Shelter modules** — self-erecting; 400 sq ft; climate-controlled; 72-hour setup-free deployment
- **Infrastructure bundle** — water purification, solar power array, communications hub, and medical station in each drop package
- **911Hub integration** — each deployed site becomes a 911Hub emergency node; satellite uplink for comms
- **Scalable** — single family shelter to 500-person camp configuration via modular linking

---

## Target Deployments

- Hurricane, flood, and earthquake disaster response
- Military forward operating base (FOB) rapid establishment
- Refugee and displaced persons camp infrastructure
- Remote infrastructure gap bridging (rural, island, polar)
