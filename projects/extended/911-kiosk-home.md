# 911InformaticsKioskHome

**"From Preparedness to Peace of Mind"**

The 911 Informatics Kiosk™ is a wall-mounted, modular kiosk system designed for seamless integration into residential homes, apartment buildings, schools, and community centers. It serves as the home node of the 911 Emergency Medical Informatics ecosystem — providing AED access, medication storage, first aid guidance, and direct 911Hub connectivity.

---

## Core Technology

- **AED integration** — built-in AED with AI-guided CPR instructions via voice and display
- **Medication cabinet** — climate-controlled; tracks expiration; network-verified access for controlled substances
- **Biometric intake station** — VitalsOne companion reader; SpO2, BP, glucose for home health monitoring
- **911Hub direct link** — one-touch emergency escalation; automatic vitals and location transmission
- **AI health assistant** — symptom triage, medication reminder, and 911 escalation recommendation
- **FHIR R4** — health data sharing with primary care provider

---

## Target Locations

- Single-family homes (elderly, high-risk households)
- Apartment and condo common areas
- HOA community centers
- Schools and daycare facilities
