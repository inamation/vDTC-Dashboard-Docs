# Xpod

**xPod™ Mini & Trauma Systems — Clarity out of the Chaos**

xPod™ is a next-generation field-deployable X-ray ecosystem designed for emergency medicine, military, and pre-hospital care. The platform delivers diagnostic imaging at the point of injury — eliminating transport-before-imaging delays that cost lives in trauma, combat, and mass casualty scenarios.

---

## Core Technology

- **xPod Mini** — portable flat-panel detector; battery-powered; <8kg total system weight
- **Trauma configuration** — larger detector array for thoracic/abdominal imaging in field trauma bays
- **AI image interpretation** — pneumothorax, hemothorax, fracture, and foreign body detection in <30 seconds
- **911Hub integration** — DICOM image relay to receiving hospital; radiologist pre-alert via MQTT
- **Ruggedized enclosure** — IP54; MIL-STD-810 shock and vibration rated

---

## Target Deployments

- Tactical combat casualty care (TCCC)
- EMS trauma response
- Disaster field hospitals
- Remote wilderness and expedition medicine
