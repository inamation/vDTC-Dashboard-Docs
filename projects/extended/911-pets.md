# 911Pets

**Emergency Intelligence for Every Life You Love.**

911 Pets & Vets delivers emergency readiness intelligence for animals and their owners — integrating pet biometrics, veterinary emergency routing, and 911Hub awareness into a consumer-first product stack.

---

## Product Stack

### 911 Pets Home
Purpose-built for families and elderly pet owners — home monitoring for pets with AI health alerts and automatic veterinary escalation.

- **Smart collar** — heart rate, temperature, GPS, activity; BLE + LTE-M
- **AI health engine** — anomaly detection for cardiac events, heat stroke, toxin ingestion patterns
- **Emergency routing** — automatic nearest 24-hour emergency vet alert with pet vitals and owner info

### 911 Pets Field
For working animals, K9 units, and service animals in field environments.

- **Ruggedized vest sensor array** — SpO2, core temp, impact detection
- **Handler alert** — haptic + audio; GPS track for separated animals
- **911Hub integration** — K9 vitals relayed to incident command during operations

### 911 Vets Network
Veterinary emergency coordination platform — the "911Hub for animal emergency care."

- **PSAP-adjacent routing** — 911 calls involving animals flagged and routed to nearest vet ER
- **Telemedicine bridge** — immediate vet consultation on animal emergency calls
