# 911InformaticsKioskAuto

**911 Informatics Kiosk™ Auto — Mobile Modular ICU Command**

A groundbreaking platform that transforms vehicles into mobile, modular ICU command stations. Designed for battlefield, civilian, and disaster deployment — the system converts any SUV, van, or truck into a full emergency medical informatics node.

---

## Core Technology

- **Vehicle-mount system** — universal rail mount; powers from vehicle 12V/48V or onboard battery pack
- **Integrated display array** — 3× touchscreen panels; patient vitals, incident map, comm channels
- **BioCode Red dock** — onboard blood analysis with FHIR R4 relay to receiving hospital
- **911Hub command node** — full PSAP integration; radio bridge; MQTT data relay
- **Telemedicine link** — encrypted video consultation with ED physician; AI pre-diagnosis assist

---

## Target Vehicles

- EMS supervisory and command vehicles
- Law enforcement mobile command
- Military medic vehicle (HMMWV, JLTV)
- Disaster response fleet vehicles
