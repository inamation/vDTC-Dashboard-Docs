# DeltaEMTCapsuleDrone

**ΔEMT™ Capsule Drone — Air Mobility Meets Medical Precision**

The ΔEMT™ Capsule Drone is a next-generation autonomous medical evacuation and critical care platform designed to bridge the gap between injury and definitive care. It delivers medications, blood products, and AED capability — and can extract a single patient from locations inaccessible to ground EMS.

---

## Core Technology

- **Payload capsule** — climate-controlled; carries blood products, epinephrine, TXA, airway kit, AED
- **Patient extraction configuration** — single-occupant litter frame; 250 lb lift capacity; autonomous flight
- **AI navigation** — obstacle avoidance; GPS + LiDAR; FAA Part 107 BVLOS compliance framework
- **911Hub integration** — dispatch via MQTT; en-route vitals relay from onboard sensors; hospital pre-alert
- **Endurance** — 45-minute flight time (delivery config); 20-minute (patient extraction)

---

## Target Use Cases

- Rural trauma and cardiac arrest response (AED delivery in <4 min)
- Wildfire and flood evacuation
- Maritime medical emergency
- Military CASEVAC in contested environments
