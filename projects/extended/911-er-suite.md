# 911ERsuite

**Urgent Care That Actually Handles Emergencies.**

A distributed, small-footprint, full emergency and surgical preparedness network designed to replace traditional urgent care and offload ERs. 911 ER Suites are modular facilities deployable in retail corridors, transportation hubs, stadiums, and underserved communities — each fully integrated with 911Hub command.

---

## Core Concept

- **Modular build-out** — 800–2,400 sq ft configurations; emergency bay, surgical bay, imaging, pharmacy
- **911Hub command integration** — each Suite is a registered PSAP node; receives routed 911 calls for medical assist
- **Telemedicine backbone** — real-time specialist consultation via secure video; AI-assisted diagnosis
- **Xpod imaging** — integrated field X-ray for trauma assessment without hospital transfer
- **BioCode Red** — onsite blood panel in <4 minutes; results to 911Hub and referring hospital

---

## Target Locations

- Retail anchor spaces (post-retail vacancy conversion)
- Airport and transit terminals
- University campuses
- Rural critical access communities
- Sports and entertainment venues
