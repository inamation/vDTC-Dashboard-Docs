# GyroScopicSurgery

**Non-Invasive Solutions That Get Right to the Point — Patent Pending**

GyroScopic Surgery™ pioneers a new era in non-invasive surgical access by utilizing rotational torque and integrated imaging guidance to reach deep anatomical targets without conventional incision. Designed for trauma, oncology, and neurosurgery applications where minimizing tissue disruption is critical.

---

## Core Technology

- **Rotational access instrument** — gyroscopic torque-driven insertion; navigates curved anatomical pathways
- **AI imaging guidance** — real-time CT/MRI fusion with instrument tip tracking; haptic boundary feedback
- **Therapeutic payload delivery** — biopsy, ablation, drug delivery, and clot retrieval via instrument tip
- **AortaPoint integration** — shared AI ultrasound guidance platform for vascular access applications
- **Surgeon console** — 3D holographic display; motion-scaled controls; tremor filtration

---

## Target Applications

- Deep tumor biopsy and ablation
- Intracerebral hemorrhage evacuation
- Spinal and intervertebral disc access
- Vascular foreign body retrieval
