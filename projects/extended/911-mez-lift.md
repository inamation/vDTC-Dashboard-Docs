# 911MEZ-LIFT

**Vertical Rescue When Space Disappears.**

911 Mez-Lift is a powered, narrow-access rescue stretcher engineered for mezzanines, catwalks, industrial stairs, ships, warehouses, and legacy industrial infrastructure where standard stretchers cannot operate. Designed for industrial rescue teams, fire departments, and maritime emergency response.

---

## Core Technology

- **Narrow-profile powered stretcher** — fits 24" stairwells and industrial catwalks; motorized descent/ascent
- **Patient stabilization** — full-body restraint system; C-spine immobilization integrated
- **911Hub integration** — rescue team coordination via MQTT; patient vitals relay during extraction
- **Battery-powered drive** — 4-hour operation; regenerative braking on descent
- **Weight capacity** — 400 lbs operational load

---

## Target Deployments

- Industrial manufacturing facilities
- Warehouse mezzanine rescue
- Maritime vessel medical evacuation
- Legacy building stairwell rescue (pre-ADA structures)
- Mining and underground facility emergency egress
