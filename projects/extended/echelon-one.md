# EchelonOne

**Designed for Distributed Auditory Processing — Patent Pending**

Echelon One is a next-generation ultra-high-fidelity headphone platform engineered to transcend conventional audio reproduction. Designed for audiophiles, studio engineers, and tactical operators who require spatial precision, dynamic range, and hearing protection in a single system.

---

## Core Technology

- **Distributed driver array** — 6 custom dynamic drivers per ear; individual DSP per driver; full-range planar response
- **AI spatial processing** — real-time HRTF personalization; head-tracked 3D audio; environment-adaptive EQ
- **Active hearing protection** — dual-mode: audiophile listening + tactical pass-through with suppression above 85 dB
- **Distributed processing node** — onboard Cortex-M7; local AI inference for spatial audio without cloud dependency
- **Battery** — 40-hour playtime; wireless charging; ANODE+ cell integration
- **Materials** — aerospace-grade aluminum; memory foam + gel hybrid ear cups; IPX4

---

## Target Users

- Professional studio engineers and mastering engineers
- High-end consumer audiophiles
- Military and law enforcement tactical operators
- Competitive gaming and esports professionals
