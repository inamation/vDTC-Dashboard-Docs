# JOBSITE911

**Emergency Intelligence Where Work Happens.**

Jobsite 911™ is a modular emergency-intelligence platform that embeds advanced medical response, safety intelligence, and real-time incident management into construction sites, manufacturing plants, and industrial facilities. Every job site becomes a 911Hub-connected emergency node.

---

## Core Technology

- **Site command unit** — ruggedized tablet with 911Hub integration; incident log; crew roster and GPS
- **AED + trauma kit stations** — distributed across site; GPS-tagged; access triggers 911Hub alert
- **Worker wearables** — VitalsOne bands for high-risk personnel; fall detection; heat stress monitoring
- **Environmental sensors** — air quality (CO, H₂S, VOC), noise level, temperature; OSHA threshold alerts
- **AI incident response** — automatic SOP dispatch based on incident type; crew accountability check-in
- **LOTObuddyCAM integration** — lone worker monitoring for maintenance shutdowns

---

## Target Markets

- Commercial construction sites
- Manufacturing and processing plants
- Mining and extraction operations
- Oil and gas field operations
- Utility and infrastructure maintenance
