# BioCore

**Adaptive Structural Implant Platform — Patent Pending**

BioCore™ is the next-generation adaptive structural implant platform designed to replace rigid, pre-manufactured orthopedic implants with minimally invasive, in-situ formable constructs. The platform enables patient-specific implant geometry without CT-guided pre-operative planning or custom manufacturing lead time.

---

## Core Technology

- **In-situ adaptive material** — shape-memory polymer composite; surgeon-adjustable during implantation; locks to final geometry on thermal activation
- **Structural reinforcement** — carbon fiber + biocompatible polymer matrix; compressive strength exceeding cortical bone
- **Bioactive surface** — hydroxyapatite coating with controlled BMP-2 release; promotes osseointegration within 4–6 weeks
- **AI surgical guidance** — intraoperative imaging overlay; implant placement optimization via AortaPoint-derived imaging platform
- **Bioresorbable option** — degradable variant for pediatric and temporary fixation applications

---

## Target Applications

- Spinal fusion and disc replacement
- Long bone fracture fixation
- Joint reconstruction (hip, knee, shoulder)
- Craniofacial and maxillofacial reconstruction
- Pediatric orthopedics (bioresorbable variant)
