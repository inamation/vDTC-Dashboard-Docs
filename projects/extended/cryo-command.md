# CryoCommand

**Wildfire Suppression and Rescue Ecosystem — Patent Pending**

CryoCommand™ establishes global dominance in wildfire combat and tactical rescue through cryogenic suppression technologies that neutralize fires faster, safer, and at lower environmental cost than conventional retardant systems.

---

## Core Technology

- **Cryogenic suppression agents** — liquid CO₂ and nitrogen-based delivery; rapid thermal quench without water damage
- **Drone-mounted delivery array** — swarm-capable; autonomous target acquisition via thermal imaging
- **Ground unit** — pressurized backpack system for hotshot crews; 30% lighter than SCBA + hose equivalent
- **CryoCommand Center** — incident command integration with real-time fire perimeter mapping and suppression coverage analytics
- **911Hub integration** — incident data relay to PSAP and ICS; crew GPS and vitals via VitalsOne

---

## Target Deployments

- Wildland-urban interface (WUI) fire response
- Structural fire suppression (reduced water damage)
- Industrial and chemical fire response
- Military base and airfield fire suppression
