# AireCabineSignature

**Refrigeration. Refined.**

AireCabiné Signature™ is a next-generation integrated climate and sanitation cabinet system engineered to replace outdated kitchen appliances and workspace refrigeration with a single precision-controlled unit. Designed for residential luxury, commercial kitchens, medical storage, and pharmaceutical cold chain.

---

## Core Technology

- **Integrated climate zones** — independent temperature control per compartment (fresh, frozen, beverage, pharmaceutical)
- **UV-C sanitation cycle** — automatic pathogen elimination without chemical agents
- **AI inventory management** — computer vision shelf tracking; expiration alerts; grocery list generation
- **Precision pharmaceutical mode** — 2–8°C validated storage with continuous monitoring and compliance logging
- **Silent operation** — magnetic linear compressor; <18 dB operating noise
- **ANODE+ integration** — optional ANODE+ backup power for uninterrupted cold chain during outages

---

## Target Markets

- Residential luxury segment
- Commercial restaurant and hospitality
- Medical office and clinical storage
- Pharmaceutical cold chain (last-mile)
