# ANODE+

**The Power Synapse — From Consumer to Grid**

ANODE+ is a revolutionary electron-driven energy module scalable from consumer electronics to grid-level storage. It introduces a breakthrough architecture in power system design — enabling high-density energy storage, rapid charge cycles, and intelligent load management across the full power spectrum.

---

## Core Technology

- **Advanced electrode architecture** — proprietary composite anode material; 3× energy density vs. standard Li-ion at equivalent volume
- **Rapid charge** — 80% charge in <12 minutes (consumer config); <45 minutes (grid module)
- **Thermal management** — active phase-change cooling; safe operation from -40°C to +85°C
- **Intelligent BMS** — AI-driven battery management system; predictive cell balancing; failure prediction
- **Scalable modules** — 100Wh (consumer) → 500kWh (grid); modular stacking architecture

---

## Target Applications

- **Consumer** — smartphone, laptop, power tool replacement cells
- **EV** — drop-in range extension module for EV platforms
- **Industrial** — UPS, backup power for data centers and medical facilities
- **Grid** — utility-scale storage for renewable energy integration
- **vDTC integration** — Luminary data center and IronShield grid storage
