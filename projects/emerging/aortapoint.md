# AortaPoint

**Next-Generation Circulatory Access and Stabilization System — Patent Pending**

AortaPoint is a minimally invasive circulatory access platform for emergency and surgical settings — targeting rapid aortic occlusion, resuscitative endovascular balloon occlusion of the aorta (REBOA), and hemorrhage control in penetrating trauma and non-compressible truncal hemorrhage.

---

## Clinical Problem

- **Non-compressible truncal hemorrhage** is the leading cause of preventable death in trauma — responsible for 80% of military combat deaths from hemorrhage and ~50% of civilian trauma hemorrhage deaths
- Current REBOA devices (Prytime ER-REBOA, Frontline Medical ER-REBOA) require a 7-Fr arterial sheath, fluoroscopy guidance, and a trained interventionalist
- **Gap:** No AI-guided, ultrasound-integrated REBOA system enables deployment by a paramedic or general surgeon without fluoroscopy

---

## Core Technology

- **AortaPoint catheter** — 4-Fr compatible (smaller than current 7-Fr systems); compliant balloon; atraumatic tip
- **AI placement guidance** — real-time ultrasound fusion with AI aortic segmentation; auditory and haptic feedback for correct zone placement (Zone I / Zone III per ER-REBOA protocol)
- **Pressure feedback** — distal limb pressure monitoring for ischemia management; auto-deflation protocol
- **AortaPoint Console** — portable, battery-powered; ultrasound probe integration; Jetson Orin NX AI inference
- **EMS integration** — 911Hub MQTT for real-time pressure data and hospital pre-alert
