# Emerging Portfolio

vDTC's emerging portfolio encompasses products in concept-to-prototype stage across emergency response, consumer health, food science, and edge hardware. These projects share the vDTC design philosophy: hardware + AI + data — built for deployment in environments where standard consumer technology fails.

## Pipeline Criteria

Projects enter the emerging portfolio when they meet at least two of:
- A specific unmet clinical or operational need with quantified patient / operator impact
- A technology pathway that leverages existing vDTC capabilities (AI, edge hardware, MQTT/FHIR stack)
- A regulatory pathway with established predicate (FDA 510(k) or De Novo)
- A market size that justifies development investment ($200M+ TAM)

Projects graduate to primary portfolio status when a working prototype is validated and an FDA pre-submission meeting is scheduled.
