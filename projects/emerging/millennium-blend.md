# MillenniumBlend

**Reversing Malnutrition with Food Science**

MillenniumBlend is a precision nutritional platform targeting malnutrition, cachexia, and metabolic disease — combining AI-formulated macronutrient/micronutrient blends with continuous biomarker monitoring. The platform bridges food science and clinical nutrition, with an initial focus on pediatric malnutrition in resource-limited settings and cancer cachexia in oncology.

---

## Clinical Problem

- **Global malnutrition:** 733 million people face hunger globally; 45% of deaths in children under 5 are malnutrition-linked (WHO, 2024)
- **Cancer cachexia:** Affects 50–80% of cancer patients; responsible for 20% of cancer deaths; no FDA-approved pharmacological treatment
- **Existing solutions:** Ready-to-use therapeutic foods (RUTF, e.g., Plumpy'Nut) are standardized — no personalization for individual metabolic needs
- **Gap:** No platform integrates real-time biomarker monitoring with AI-formulated therapeutic nutrition

---

## Core Technology

- **AI formulation engine** — LLM + metabolic modeling to generate individualized macronutrient/micronutrient blend targets based on biomarkers (albumin, prealbumin, CRP, BMI trajectory)
- **MillenniumBlend sachets** — precision-manufactured powdered nutrition; GMP-certified production; WHO/UNICEF RUTF specification-compatible
- **BioSync wristband** — BLE hydration + skin temperature + glucose trending (non-invasive); feeds formulation adjustment loop
- **Clinical dashboard** — FHIR R4 integration; nutrition team visualization; compliance tracking
