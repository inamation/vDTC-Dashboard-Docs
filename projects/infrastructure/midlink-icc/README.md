# Midlink-ICC — Mixed-Use Tower

**15-story mixed-use development near the airport corridor, designed for FAA obstruction compliance and integrated with the vDTC infrastructure campus.**

Midlink-ICC is a commercial real estate development anchoring the physical footprint of the vDTC infrastructure hub. The tower combines technology-forward office space, co-working, hospitality, and ground-floor retail — designed to attract aerospace, medical technology, and defense contractors in the airport corridor.

---

## Development Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Height | 15 stories (~195 ft AGL) |
| Total GFA | ~185,000 sq ft |
| Site | Near airport, Battle Creek / Fort Custer corridor, MI |
| FAA Compliance | FAA Form 7460-1 (Notice of Proposed Construction), AC 70/7460-1L obstruction lighting |
| Zoning | Mixed-use commercial — Type I-B construction |
