# Infrastructure Division

The vDTC Infrastructure Division develops physical and digital infrastructure that enables the long-term operation of the portfolio — energy independence, data sovereignty, and compute capacity at the scale that medical AI and quantum systems require.

---

## Projects

| Project | Type | Stage |
|---|---|---|
| **Midlink-ICC** | 15-story mixed-use tower | Site design / FAA compliance |
| **IronShield** | Small Modular Reactor (SMR) | NRC licensing process (3–7 year timeline) |
| **Luminary Architecture** | Hyperscale data center powered by IronShield | Design phase |
| **Axiom Quantum** | Quantum computing platform integrated into Luminary | Concept / partner evaluation |

---

## Strategic Rationale

The medical AI systems vDTC is building — real-time ECG inference, surgical planning, neurovascular imaging, multi-agent AI orchestration — require massive compute that cannot depend on commercial cloud availability or third-party energy pricing. IronShield → Luminary → Axiom Quantum forms a vertically integrated compute stack:

```
IronShield SMR (carbon-free baseload power)
      │
      ▼
Luminary Data Center (hospital-grade uptime, 40–200 MW capacity)
      │
      ▼
Axiom Quantum (quantum AI acceleration for drug discovery, logistics, cryptography)
      │
      ▼
vDTC Product Portfolio (911 Ecosystem, CardioPoint, NeuroSeal, Anatomic AI...)
```

Midlink-ICC provides the physical co-location, revenue base, and FAA-compliant vertical presence near the airport corridor that anchors the infrastructure hub.
