# NeuroSeal

**AI-guided neurovascular sealing system for hemorrhagic stroke and traumatic brain injury.**

NeuroSeal is a minimally invasive neurovascular intervention platform combining real-time imaging, AI surgical guidance, and bioresorbable sealing technology to address hemorrhagic stroke and traumatic intracranial bleeding — conditions where minutes determine survivability and neurological outcome.

---

## Clinical Problem

- Hemorrhagic stroke kills or disables 800,000 Americans per year
- Traumatic brain injury (TBI) causes 220,000 hospitalizations annually
- Current intervention (craniotomy, endovascular coiling) requires large OR teams and extended procedure time
- No FDA-cleared AI-guided field stabilization exists for neurovascular hemorrhage

---

## Core Technology

- **Bioresorbable sealant delivery** — catheter-deployed sealant formulation (PEG-based hydrogel) that conforms to vessel wall geometry
- **AI imaging guidance** — real-time CT/MRI fusion with hemorrhage segmentation and delivery path planning
- **Pressure feedback loop** — intracranial pressure monitoring during delivery, closed-loop sealant volume control
- **NeuroSeal Console** — portable imaging workstation with on-device AI (Jetson AGX Orin)
